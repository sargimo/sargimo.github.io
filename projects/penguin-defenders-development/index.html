Our Mission

Penguin Defenders has pledged to protect penguins on the West Coast of New Zealand and ensure a brighter future. Care for the land, care for the wildlife, and care for the community.

The
Plight
of the
Penguin

Over the last 30 years, we have seen the number of mainland penguins on the West Coast plummet. There were once estimated to be between 2 and 3 thousand of our flappy friends, and today that number is estimated to be closer to 2 to 3 hundred. What is causing it? As it turns out, a lot of things.

Through research and education, we have seen a positive trend since we began, with estimated penguin numbers being about 10% higher than when we started. We have a lot of work to do and are fully committed to a better future.
