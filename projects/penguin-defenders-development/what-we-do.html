Penguin

Penguin Defenders has pledged to protect penguins on the West Coast of New Zealand and ensure a brighter future. Care for the land, care for the wildlife, and care for the community.

We believe change starts with education and community. The research we regularly fund is constantly uncovering new paths for us to go down and finding the best ways for us to help. It is then our job to come together and make real change within our communities.

Progress

The estimated penguin population has gone up around 10% since we began. While we are thrilled to see positive change, we are aware that there is still a lot of work to do. We are committed to long term goals and a permanent better future.

How can you help?

84% of our donation money goes straight to research and programs. It is crucial for our success to continue funding this resarch and finding the path to a better future.

Education &
Community

We believe strongly in community and education. We have had a fantastic relationship with a number of local schools for many years now. These relationships have had a huge impact on our penguin population, with schools collectively putting in over 300 hours a year into our programs.

We regularly make visits to schools and other local groups, connecting and educating about what small things we can do to preserve our backyard for generations to come.
